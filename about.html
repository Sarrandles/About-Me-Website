The Basics

Alright, well, writing about yourself is difficult and awkward, so buckle up! My name is Sarah Randles and I was born and raised in Detroit, Michigan *woot woot*! I enjoy burritos, dismantling the patriarchy, playing video games, and fighting live bears for money. I've worked at two libraries, a bookstore, and schools over the years and I'm the happiest when I have a book in my hand. Do you like lists? I like lists.
